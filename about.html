Our Story

Garage Marketing Solutions was founded in 2018 by a team of digital marketing experts who saw a glaring gap in the market. While thousands of garages across the UK struggled to attract customers online, generic marketing agencies failed to understand the automotive industry's unique needs.

We started with a simple mission: help independent garages compete with large franchise dealerships by leveraging the power of digital marketing. What began as a small team of three has grown into a full-service agency serving over 250 garage businesses across the United Kingdom.

Today, we're proud to be the UK's leading digital marketing agency specializing exclusively in automotive businesses. Our campaigns have generated over ¬£2.5 million in revenue for our clients, and we're just getting started.
